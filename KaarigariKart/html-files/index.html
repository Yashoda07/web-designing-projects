Welcome to KaarigariKart

KarigariKart is a platform to showcase the incredible craftsmanship of Indian artisans. From weaving in Varanasi to pottery in Rajasthan to embriodery in Himachal and Punjab to warli in Maharashtra, every art tells a story.

KarigariKart is a celebration of creativity, culture, and craftsmanship. Built with love by an artist, for the artists. this platform brings together the vibrant world of Indian handicrafts under one digital roof. KarigariKart supports local talent, gives artisans a voice, and makes traditional art forms accessible to the world. Every product has a story, and every story begins with a creator like you.

At KarigariKart, we believe that every handmade piece carries the soul of its maker. Our mission is not only to connect buyers with authentic crafts but also to empower artisans by giving them a platform to reach beyond local boundaries. Whether you are looking for a unique gift, a piece of tradition, or a touch of culture for your home, KarigariKart brings you closer to the heart of India’s artistry.
